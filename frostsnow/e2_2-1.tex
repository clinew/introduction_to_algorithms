\section*{Exercise 2.2-1}

Discarding all lower-order polynominals and all coefficients from:

\begin{eqnarray*}
	\frac{n^3}{1000} - 100 n^2 - 100 n + 3
\end{eqnarray*}
...gives:
\begin{eqnarray*}
	\Theta(n^3)
\end{eqnarray*}
