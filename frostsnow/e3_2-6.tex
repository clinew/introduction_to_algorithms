\section*{Exercise 3.2-6}

By definition of the quadratic formula:
\begin{eqnarray*}
a x^2 + b x + c & = & 0 \\
x & = & \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
\end{eqnarray*}
Solving the equation $x^2 = x + 1$ gives:
\begin{eqnarray*}
	x^2 & = & x + 1 \\
	x^2 - x - 1 & = & 0 \\
	\frac{1 \pm \sqrt{1 + 4}}{2} & = & 0 \\
	\frac{1 \pm \sqrt{5}}{2} & = & \{1.61803, -0.61803 \}
\end{eqnarray*}
